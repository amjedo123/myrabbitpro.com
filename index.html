Features

Everything your rabbitry needs

Built by a rabbit breeder, for rabbit breeders. Every feature comes from real experience managing a working rabbitry.

🐇
Complete rabbit profiles
Track every rabbit with tattoo ID, DOB, breed, weight history, health records and photos. Generate professional pedigree certificates in one tap.
❤️
Smart breeding tracker
Record a breeding and RabbitPro automatically calculates pregnancy check, nest box installation, and kindling dates. Never miss a critical date again.
🥚
Litter management
Track kits from birth to tattoo day. Log individual weights, monitor growth charts, and promote kits to permanent records at weaning with one tap.
✅
Automatic task reminders
Tasks for pregnancy checks, nest boxes, weaning and tattooing are generated automatically from your breeding records. Your dashboard shows what needs attention today.
📊
Financial tracking
Track expenses by category, record sales, and generate monthly P&L reports. Know exactly what your rabbitry costs and earns.
📄
Professional PDF reports
Generate pedigree certificates, rabbit biographies for buyers, health reports, breeding performance analysis, and financial summaries — all as shareable PDFs.
☁️
Real-time sync
Log a kindling on your phone in the barn and see it instantly on your office computer. Works across all your devices simultaneously.
🔒
Your data is private
Each account is completely isolated. No one can see your rabbitry data — not other users, not advertisers. Your breeding records stay yours.
🌿
Feed log
Track daily feeding, feed types and amounts, and water checks. Build a complete record of your feeding program over time.
